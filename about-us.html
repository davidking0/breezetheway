About MARTA

Reliable

Breeze The Way aims to be a digital platform that provides reliable information to MARTA riders through a convenient vehicle. There are enough uncertainties in life, and, at Breeze The Way, we aim not the be one of them.

Connected

Breeze The Way seeks to inspire consistent ridership by offering a transparent view of your rider history. We want to be a resource to help users and members of the community be most effective with their finances by choosing MARTA as their transportation solution.

Organized

Breeze The Way seeks to eliminate the confusing system of delayed communication between MARTA and its users in order to help riders get to their destinations in a safe and timely manner.

Effective

Breeze The Way seeks to provide riders with accesibility to the resources they need, including managing their breeze card account, engaging with pertinent MARTA news, providing awareness of MARTA initiatives, and offering incentives for them to engage with the community around them.
